This roast chicken is crispy on the outside, juicy on the inside, and infused with aromatic garlic and lemon. You can serve it with roasted vegetables or mashed potatoes for a complete meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (about 4-6 lbs)
  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 4 garlic cloves, sliced
  • 3 sprigs fresh rosemary
  • 1 lemon, halved
  • Salt and pepper
  • Kitchen twine (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 450¬∞F (230¬∞C). Place a rack in the center.
  2. Prep the chicken: Remove giblets, trim excess fat, rinse and dry thoroughly.
  3. Make garlic butter: Melt butter in a saucepan, then add garlic and rosemary. Let sit for 5 minutes.
  4. Stuff the cavity: Place garlic slices, rosemary, and lemon halves inside the chicken.
  5. Season: Brush melted butter all over the chicken. Generously season with salt and pepper, including inside the cavity.
  6. Tie the legs: Use kitchen twine to tie the legs together for even cooking.
  7. Roast: Place chicken in a roasting pan. Roast for 1 hour, basting every 15 minutes with pan juices. Rotate the pan each time for even browning.
  8. Check doneness: Internal temperature should reach 165¬∞F (74¬∞C) in the breast (avoid touching bone).
  9. Rest and serve: Let the chicken rest for 10 minutes before slicing.
